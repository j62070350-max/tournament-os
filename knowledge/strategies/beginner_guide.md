# Beginner Guide to Mech Arena

## Core Concepts

### 1. Positioning is everything
Movement and cover matter more than firepower in the early game.
- Stay near walls and obstacles that block incoming fire
- Never stand in the open when enemies have line of sight
- Use buildings to break aggro and regen between fights

### 2. Learn one mech first
Pick one mech and learn it deeply before branching out.
- **Best starter mechs:** Atlas (durable, forgiving), Falcon (fast, teaches positioning)
- Understand your ability and use it — abilities win fights

### 3. Understand weapon ranges
Every weapon has an optimal range. Using it outside that range wastes damage.
- Orkans: get close (under 350m)
- Scourge: mid-range (150–450m)
- Pulsar: stay far (400–700m)

### 4. Use ability on cooldown
Don't hold your ability waiting for the "perfect moment" — use it frequently.
- Abilities off cooldown = wasted potential
- Exception: save ability for a certain kill or guaranteed escape

### 5. Objectives win games
Killing mechs earns points, but capturing objectives wins matches.
- Always contest the center zone in Deathmatch variants
- A 3v3 objective fight beats a 1v1 kill hunt

## Progression Tips
- Complete daily missions for the fastest resource gain
- Upgrade your primary mech to max before buying a second
- Watch the top players in replays to learn positioning habits

## Common Beginner Mistakes
- Standing still while shooting (easiest target to hit)
- Using ability too early in a fight and having nothing left when pressured
- Upgrading multiple mechs simultaneously instead of focusing one
- Ignoring the map minimap — always watch where enemies are
