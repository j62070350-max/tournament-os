# Counter Guide: How to Beat Any Mech

## Countering Heavy Mechs (Atlas, Titan, Bulwark)

**Problem:** Too much HP and armor to burst down.

**Solution:**
- Use sustained DPS weapons (Scourge, Pulsar) to grind through their HP
- Flank to their rear — many heavies have slower turn speeds
- Coordinate with teammates: two mechs focusing one heavy drops it fast
- Kite: stay at range, deny close-range approach

**Best Counter Mechs:** Ares (range), Fujin (sustained DPS at range)

---

## Countering Flankers (Falcon, Mauler)

**Problem:** Too fast to track, burst kills before you can react.

**Solution:**
- Spread weapons (Orkan) catch fast-moving mechs in the explosion radius
- Pre-position in chokepoints where they must come to you
- Save your ability for when they use theirs — counter-engage after their Evade expires
- Stick with teammates — flankers struggle against grouped targets

**Best Counter Mechs:** Atlas (high HP outlasts burst), Titan (shield negates the burst)

---

## Countering Snipers (Ares at long range)

**Problem:** They deal massive damage before you can reach them.

**Solution:**
- Use terrain — move between cover, never cross open ground
- Rush during their reload/cooldown window
- Fast mechs (Falcon) can close the gap before Ares repositions
- Mirror with your own Ares if you can match their position

**Best Counter Mechs:** Falcon, Mauler (reach them before they reset)

---

## General Counter Tips
1. **Identify the enemy composition** at match start from the loading screen
2. **Pick weapons that counter the most common threat** on the enemy team
3. **Always engage on your terms** — initiate only when you have HP advantage or ability ready
4. **Retreat and heal** rather than fighting to the death when low
