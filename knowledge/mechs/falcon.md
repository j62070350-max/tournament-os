# Falcon

## Overview
Falcon is a high-speed flanker mech that excels at hit-and-run tactics. Its Evade ability makes it temporarily untargetable, enabling aggressive repositioning.

## Stats
- **Class:** Light
- **HP:** 90,000
- **Armor:** 20
- **Speed:** 76 km/h
- **Ability:** Evade — dashes and becomes untargetable for 2 seconds
- **Ability Cooldown:** 15 seconds

## Strengths
- Fastest mech in the game — exceptional map traversal and flanking
- Evade provides a near-guaranteed escape from burst damage
- Excellent at targeting isolated or low-HP enemies

## Weaknesses
- Very low HP — dies quickly if caught out of position
- Dependent on ability timing; baited Evades leave it exposed
- Less effective against spread weapons like Orkans at close range

## Recommended Weapons
- **Dual Orkans** — devastating close-range burst during ability windows
- **Spiral + Orkan** — consistent close-mid pressure
- **Scourge** — sustained DPS for sustained engagements

## Best Pilots
- **Ace** — reduces Evade cooldown, enabling more frequent repositioning
- **Viper** — increases speed further, maximizing flanking potential

## Counter
- Spread weapons (Orkan, Scatter Gun) are effective at close range
- Predict Evade direction and pre-position
- Atlas or other high-HP mechs can trade favorably if Falcon's Evade is burned

## Tier
**A-tier** — strong solo-queue carry in the right hands
