# Ares

## Overview
Ares is a legendary sniper mech designed for long-range dominance. Its Overclock ability amplifies weapon damage significantly, making it one of the highest burst damage mechs in the game.

## Stats
- **Class:** Medium
- **HP:** 140,000
- **Armor:** 35
- **Speed:** 44 km/h
- **Ability:** Overclock — massively boosts weapon damage for a short duration
- **Ability Duration:** 4 seconds
- **Ability Cooldown:** 18 seconds

## Strengths
- Highest single-ability burst damage potential in the game
- Excellent range — can safely engage from positions most mechs cannot reach
- Medium speed lets it reposition after firing

## Weaknesses
- Lower HP than other legendary mechs
- Ability timing is critical — mistimed Overclock wastes enormous damage potential
- Struggles in close range due to weapon choice constraints

## Recommended Weapons
- **Dual Pulsar** — exceptional long-range burst synergy with Overclock
- **Punisher + Scourge** — consistent long-range DPS
- **Cryo Cannon** — freezes enemies for guaranteed Overclock follow-up

## Best Pilots
- **Panther** — extends Overclock effectiveness
- **Ghost** — provides stealth between shots

## Counter
- Rush with fast mechs during Ares' cooldown window
- Falcon or Mauler can close the gap before Ares repositions
- Use terrain and obstacles to block line of sight

## Tier
**S-tier** — dominant in open-map long-range meta
