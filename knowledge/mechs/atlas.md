# Atlas

## Overview
Atlas is a heavy-class mech built for sustained frontline combat. High HP and armor make it a natural tank, but its low speed requires careful positioning.

## Stats
- **Class:** Heavy
- **HP:** 220,000
- **Armor:** 60
- **Speed:** 28 km/h
- **Ability:** Iron Fist — briefly boosts damage output and reduces incoming damage
- **Ability Duration:** 5 seconds
- **Ability Cooldown:** 20 seconds

## Strengths
- Exceptional durability — among the highest base HP in the game
- Ability provides a strong burst window for aggressive pushes
- Works well in close-to-mid range fights where it can absorb punishment

## Weaknesses
- Slowest movement speed in the Heavy class
- Vulnerable to long-range sniping from Ares or Fujin
- Ability window is short — poor usage wastes the cooldown

## Recommended Weapons
- **Dual Plasma Cannons** — maximizes burst during Iron Fist window
- **Orkan + Spiral** — consistent mid-range pressure
- **Scourge** — sustained DPS that fits longer fights

## Best Pilots
- **Sarge** — increases durability during ability
- **Unit 2-B** — boosts damage output to amplify Iron Fist

## Counter
- Use fast flankers (Mauler, Falcon) to circle Atlas and exploit its low turn speed
- Ares at long range can safely chip Atlas down before it reaches you
- Avoid fighting Atlas in chokepoints where its durability negates your mobility advantage

## Tier
**A-tier** — strong frontline anchor in competitive modes
