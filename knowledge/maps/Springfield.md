# Springfield

## Overview
Springfield is a mid-sized urban map with a mix of open streets and tight building corridors. It rewards both range-focused play on the main streets and close-quarters ambushes in the alleys.

## Layout
- **Center:** Wide open main street — high ground advantage from building rooftops
- **Left flank:** Narrow alley system — favors close-range mechs
- **Right flank:** Parking structures with partial cover — good for mid-range weapons

## Key Positions
- **Roof of Central Building:** Provides line of sight across 60% of the map — prime Ares position
- **Alley Entrance (South):** Chokepoint ideal for Falcon hit-and-run plays
- **Parking Structure (Right):** Two levels — upper level has range advantage but limited escape routes

## Strategy by Role

### Frontline (Atlas, Titan)
Push center street during the opening. Control the main choke and anchor your team.

### Sniper (Ares, Fujin)
Take central building roof immediately. Maintain elevation and rotate left when pressured.

### Flanker (Falcon, Mauler)
Use the left alley to circle behind enemy snipers. Time your flank when enemies are focused on center.

## Meta Notes
- Long-range builds perform strongly in the opening — get roof control fast
- Once center is lost, fall back to alleys for close-range fights where snipers lose their advantage

## Tier for Competitive Play
**Balanced** — rewards both long-range and close-range builds depending on positioning
