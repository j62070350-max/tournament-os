# Sarge

## Overview
Sarge is a defensive pilot specializing in boosting durability during a mech's active ability window. He is a top choice for any tank or frontline mech.

## Ability
**Iron Resolve** — While the mech's active ability is in use, incoming damage is reduced by an additional 20%.

## Best Mechs
- **Atlas** — Iron Fist + Iron Resolve creates an extremely durable frontline window
- **Titan** — Shield Bash + extra damage reduction makes Titan nearly unkillable briefly
- **Bulwark** — synergy with any defensive ability mech

## Implant Synergies
- **Armor Amplifier** — stacks with Iron Resolve for maximum damage reduction
- **Regeneration Module** — heals during the reinforced ability window

## Tier
**S-tier** for heavy/tank mech builds
