# Orkan

## Overview
The Orkan is a rocket launcher weapon renowned for its explosive burst at close range. It fires a full salvo of rockets simultaneously, making it a top choice for hit-and-run builds.

## Stats
- **Type:** Rocket / Splash
- **Damage per Rocket:** ~8,000
- **Rockets per Salvo:** 8
- **Total Burst Damage:** ~64,000
- **Optimal Range:** 0–350 m
- **Max Range:** 500 m
- **Reload Time:** 10 seconds
- **Lock-on:** Not required

## Strengths
- Massive burst in a short window — ideal for ability-based mechs
- No lock-on needed — fire instantly at any target
- Splash damage hits targets behind cover partially

## Weaknesses
- Long reload makes positioning after a salvo critical
- Minimal damage outside optimal range
- Poor against fast targets that dodge the salvo arc

## Best Mech Pairings
- **Falcon** — Evade in, dump salvo, Evade out before reload
- **Atlas** — Iron Fist amplifies Orkan burst significantly
- **Mauler** — double Orkan burst eliminates most medium mechs

## Upgrade Priority
Prioritize damage upgrades. Reload reduction is secondary unless running a sustained DPS role.

## Tier
**S-tier** for close-range burst builds
