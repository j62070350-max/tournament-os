# Scourge

## Overview
The Scourge is a mid-range machine gun that provides high sustained DPS. It excels in prolonged fights where constant pressure matters more than burst.

## Stats
- **Type:** Ballistic / Auto
- **Damage per Second:** ~22,000
- **Optimal Range:** 150–450 m
- **Max Range:** 600 m
- **Magazine:** Continuous fire (overheat-limited)
- **Overheat Time:** 8 seconds
- **Cooldown:** 3 seconds

## Strengths
- Highest sustained DPS of any standard weapon
- No reload interruptions — continuous suppression
- Effective at multiple ranges unlike burst weapons

## Weaknesses
- No burst window — requires time-on-target to deal damage
- Loses effectiveness against mechs that frequently dodge or use abilities
- Overheat punishes aggressive play if not managed

## Best Mech Pairings
- **Atlas** — high HP sustains long Scourge engagements
- **Titan** — shield + Scourge creates an unkillable DPS platform
- **Bulwark** — similar synergy to Titan

## Upgrade Priority
DPS first, overheat second.

## Tier
**A-tier** across most game modes
