# Pulsar

## Overview
The Pulsar is a precision energy rifle designed for long-range damage. It fires a sustained beam that deals consistent damage without reload downtime, making it ideal for sniper roles.

## Stats
- **Type:** Energy / Beam
- **Damage per Second:** ~18,000
- **Optimal Range:** 400–700 m
- **Max Range:** 800 m
- **Overheat Time:** 6 seconds
- **Cooldown:** 4 seconds
- **Lock-on:** Not required

## Strengths
- Consistent long-range damage — no projectile travel time
- Pairs extremely well with Ares Overclock for massive burst
- No reload — only limited by overheat timer

## Weaknesses
- Falls off sharply at close range
- Poor against fast mechs that dodge the beam
- Overheat punishes sustained fire without discipline

## Best Mech Pairings
- **Ares** — Overclock multiplies Pulsar DPS by 2–3x for its duration
- **Panther** — beam pinning ability + Pulsar for consistent damage
- **Fujin** — long-range static platform benefits from Pulsar consistency

## Upgrade Priority
Damage first, then overheat threshold. Range upgrade if fighting on large maps.

## Tier
**A-tier** for long-range meta; **S-tier** with Ares Overclock
