# Armor Amplifier

## Overview
A defensive implant that permanently increases the mech's armor rating, reducing all incoming damage.

## Effect
- **Armor Bonus:** +15 armor rating
- **Effective Damage Reduction:** Scales with current armor — higher base armor = larger absolute reduction

## Best Used On
- Tank mechs (Atlas, Titan, Bulwark) where armor stacking has the largest impact
- Any mech running Sarge as pilot (stacks with Iron Resolve)

## Synergies
- **Sarge (Pilot)** — Both effects active during ability = extremely low incoming damage
- **Regeneration Module** — Armor buys time for regen to take effect

## Tier
**A-tier** for frontline builds; **S-tier** combined with Sarge on Atlas
