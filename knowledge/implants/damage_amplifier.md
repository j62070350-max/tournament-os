# Damage Amplifier

## Overview
An offensive implant that increases all weapon damage output by a flat percentage.

## Effect
- **Damage Bonus:** +12% to all weapon damage

## Best Used On
- Burst mechs (Ares, Falcon, Mauler) where the bonus multiplies high base damage
- Any mech where maximizing ability-window damage is critical

## Synergies
- **Ace (Pilot)** — More frequent ability uses means more windows to apply the damage bonus
- **Panther (Pilot)** — Extended ability duration gives more time for the bonus to accumulate

## Tier
**S-tier** for DPS and burst-focused builds
